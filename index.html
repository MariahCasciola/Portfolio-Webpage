Three encoding and decoding algorithms: Caesar Shift, Polybius Square, Substitution Ciper. Each algorithm takes in the English alphabet and utlizes it in a different way.

  • 🖤Caesar Shift will shift the alphabet over by however much a user specifies.
  • 🖤Polybius square takes each letter in the alphabet and assigns a two digit corrdinate to each letter.
  • 🖤Substitution Ciper requires a user to provide an alphabet of 26 characters to encode and decode their messages.

JavaScript ES6, DOM Manipulation, HTML5, Bootstrap, Chai, VS Code, and Git.
