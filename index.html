About Me

My name is Mariah and I am a software engineer, I never knew what I wanted to do with my life, until I fell in love with software enginneering.

The truth is, I began programming on a whim. I had grown fatigued from waiting tables and bagging groceries, and needed a way to change my life.

I enrolled into a software engineering bootcamp called Thinkful on November 14th, 2022, and graduated on April 15th, 2023.

Since graduating Thinkful, my goal has been to become a front-end developer. I love React, CSS, and Bootstrap, and I believe my artistic talent can shine through in web design.

On my own time, I enjoy drawing in ink, and painting with acrylic on canvas. I have always been very attentive to the detail of my art, and I think this translates very well into being a front-end developer. At heart I am a creator, and my software is my art.
